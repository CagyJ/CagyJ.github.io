What is the SEO? Is it important?

I think you have some thinking like them(up) if you meet SEO first time, now, let we get in the world of the SEO and learn a little basic simple knowledge.

In my opinion, it is very important for every website.

SEO is [Search . Engine . Optimization]. It means let our website improve the ranking in search engines and optimization keyword. Through the meaning, I do not think I need to explain more why its importance. In addition, our website will increase effective traffic after doing some SEO. Besides, it also supports a ecological standard of self-marketing and gain the brand revenue.

Actually, SEO includes two parts, both off-site SEO and on-site SEO.

Secondly, Internal link optimization.

The number of internal links to the page is a very important factor for search engines to review the importance of the page.
